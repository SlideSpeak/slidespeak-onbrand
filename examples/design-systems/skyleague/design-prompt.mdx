# SKYLEAGUE Presentation Master Prompt (HTML/CSS)

## Brand Essence

Create a deck that feels like SKYLEAGUE's live site: ambitious, direct, technology-led, and built
for financial-services innovation. The visual language is dark, confident, geometric, and electric:
black and purple fields, cyan technical accents, lime action moments, all-Azo-Sans typography, sharp
rectangular layouts, large circular/semicircular brand geometry, and compact business copy that
talks about making technology work.

Primary voice cues from the brand: "Turning innovation ideas into action", "Technology partner for
ambitious business", "We make the impossible, possible for you", "Making technology work for you",
"Building your competitive advantage", and "We talk business, not just code". Write in that mode:
plain, assertive, business-savvy, and execution-oriented.

## Typography

- Hero title: Azo Sans, 48-64px, weight 400, line-height 1.06, white on dark/purple. Keep it concise
  and confident.
- Action title: Azo Sans, 34-42px, weight 500, line-height 1.12. It must be a conclusion, not a
  topic label.
- Section title: Azo Sans, 44-56px, weight 500, line-height 1.04, white.
- Eyebrow: uppercase Azo Sans, 12-14px, weight 500, color cyan, line-height 1.2. Letter spacing may
  be subtle, around 0.04em, only for all-caps labels.
- Body: Azo Sans, 17-20px, weight 400, line-height 1.45. Use soft-white on dark slides and black on
  white/lime/cyan cards.
- Card titles: Azo Sans, 23-27px, weight 500, line-height 1.08.
- Data labels: Azo Sans, 12-15px, weight 400 or 500.

## Layout Grammar

- Slides are fixed 1280x720, full-bleed color fields. Avoid decorative outer borders.
- Most slides should be black or purple. White is an occasional content surface, not the default
  brand field.
- Use sharp rectangular panels with 0 border radius. The site uses square purple cards, not soft
  SaaS cards.
- Use a 1018px-ish centered content width translated to slides: x=96 to x=1184 works well, with
  generous vertical bands.
- Use asymmetric compositions: a strong left text column and a large visual/geometry on the right;
  or a two-column split with a narrow rule between them.
- One-pixel white rules are important. Use them as section dividers, column separators, footer
  lines, and chart baselines.
- Do not use drop shadows, glassmorphism, soft gradients, rounded pills, pastel tints, or
  stock-photo collage styling.

## Asset Usage

- Logo: use `assets/logo.svg`. Place it top-left on covers/dividers at about 246x40 or 220x36
  depending on slide density. Keep clear space equal to the height of the SK mark. Use only on
  dark/purple/image areas where the white wordmark is legible.
- Hero orb: use `assets/hero.png`, the glossy SKYLEAGUE purple/cyan/white geometric sphere from the
  homepage. It should be absolutely positioned, usually 520-700px square, bleeding off the right or
  bottom edge. Never place it as an inline image block.
- Circle: use `assets/circle.svg`, a purple semicircle. It can bleed from the right edge or sit
  oversized behind content on black slides.
- Icons: use `assets/icon-1.svg` and `assets/icon-2.svg` only as small 32px functional markers
  inside cards or process steps. Do not sprinkle icons across data exhibits.

## Required Slide Types

1. Cover: purple or black full bleed, exact logo top-left, cyan eyebrow, large white hero title,
   concise soft-white subtitle, lime or cyan CTA/date line, and the hero orb bleeding off the right.
   The first viewport must immediately read as SKYLEAGUE.
2. Executive Summary: black background, cyan eyebrow, action title, three to five square insight
   cards. Cards may alternate purple, cyan, lime, and white; text contrast must be exact.
3. Problem / Opportunity: large two-column slide with a white one-pixel vertical rule. Left side is
   the conclusion; right side is evidence or a compact framework.
4. Solution Architecture: use purple rectangular modules connected by cyan rules on black. Show how
   strategy, design, engineering, data, AI, compliance, and operations connect.
5. Proof / Client Success: black background with a disciplined client-logo or case-study grid. If
   client logos are not locally supplied, use neutral text labels, not invented logos.
6. Roadmap: horizontal phases using square blocks; current or recommended phase in lime, supporting
   phases in purple/cyan/white.
7. Metrics / Exhibit: use minimal charts on dark backgrounds. Highlight exactly one point in cyan or
   lime; keep axes and gridlines white at low opacity.
8. Closing / Call to Action: purple full bleed, logo, one decisive line, lime CTA block, optional
   hero orb or semicircle.

## Chart and Data Rules

- Prefer high-contrast charts on black: white labels, low-opacity white gridlines, cyan/lime
  highlights, purple secondary series.
- Use simple bars, stacked bars, roadmaps, system maps, 2x2 matrices, and maturity tables. Avoid
  ornate chart libraries.
- Direct-label series. Avoid legends unless more than three series are necessary.
- Every data slide includes a small source line in soft-white at 65% opacity.
- Data visuals should feel engineered: aligned, squared, sparse, precise.

## Copy Rules

- Use action titles: "AI-enabled onboarding can cut exception handling by 40%" instead of "AI
  onboarding".
- Keep sentences short and commercially direct.
- Use SKYLEAGUE vocabulary: innovation ideas into action, advanced technology, tailored solutions,
  competitive advantage, business results, financial-services expertise, agile execution, deep
  integration, not just code.
- Avoid generic startup fluff and abstract motivational language.

## Quality Checklist

- Logo and assets are copied locally and referenced as `assets/...`.
- The deck uses Azo Sans or the specified fallback only; no serif fonts.
- Palette is recognizably SKYLEAGUE: black/purple dominant with cyan and lime accents.
- Cards and panels are square-edged, not rounded.
- At least one slide uses the hero orb and at least one uses the semicircle geometry.
- Titles form a complete storyline when read in sequence.
- No remote images, scripts, chart libraries, or CDN dependencies except optional locally approved
  font files.
- Text never overlaps the logo, orb, or circle asset.

Return a single complete HTML file plus local copied assets beside it.
